About This Home

Tucked at the end of a quiet cul-de-sac on nearly half an acre, 6518 Day Farm Court delivers the kind of space and privacy most suburban homes only promise. There are no through-streets, no cut-through traffic, and no neighbors behind you crowding the view. Just a sprawling lot, a resort-style backyard, and 3,848 square feet of custom-built living that rewards you from the moment you walk through the door.

Step inside and you are greeted by a curved staircase, soaring two-story ceilings, and rich hardwood floors that flow through the main living areas. The open-concept layout connects a spacious living room anchored by a fireplace to a kitchen built for both everyday life and serious entertaining. Granite countertops wrap around a large island, stainless steel appliances line the workspace, and pendant lighting sets the tone overhead. A sun-filled breakfast nook tucks into the corner with a direct view of the pool, so mornings start with coffee and water.

The downstairs master suite is a true retreat. Crown molding frames the bedroom, and the en-suite bath features a soaking tub, dual vanities, and a generous walk-in closet. No stairs at the end of the day unless you want them.

Upstairs, a massive game room anchors the second floor, flanked by three additional bedrooms, each with ample closet space. Whether it is a media room, playroom, home gym, or all three, the flex space adapts to your household.

And then there is the backyard. A saltwater pool and spa with a natural rock waterfall sit center stage, surrounded by mature landscaping and open lawn on a lot that stretches to 0.43 acres. The three-car garage and upgraded HVAC round out a home that checks the boxes buyers spend months searching for.
